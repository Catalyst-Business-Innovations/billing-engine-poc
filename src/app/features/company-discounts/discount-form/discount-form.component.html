How Discounts Are Calculated

  1. Calculate all module charges (POS + TPM + Lister)
  2. Sum to get subtotal
  3. Apply discount based on type:
    • Percentage: Subtotal × (Discount % / 100)
    • Flat Amount: Subtract discount from subtotal
  4. Calculate final total (prevents negative amounts)

Discount is sent to Zoho as a separate invoice line item
